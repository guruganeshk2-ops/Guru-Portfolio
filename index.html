About Me
Crafting visuals
that tell stories

I'm Guru Ganesh K, a graphic and visual designer passionate about creating work that looks great and performs commercially.

Currently completing my MCA, I've been honing my craft across multiple design disciplines — from high-impact social media creatives and marketing materials to UI/UX digital experiences and textile surface patterns. My approach balances aesthetic precision with strategic thinking.

I believe great design isn't just beautiful — it solves real business problems. Every project starts with understanding the audience, market, and message before a single pixel is placed.

Design Philosophy
